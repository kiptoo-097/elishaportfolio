About Me

With a Master of Science in Urban Management and Development from the prestigious Institute of Housing and Urban Development Studies at Erasmus University Rotterdam, I am passionately committed to revolutionizing urban landscapes through housing and land justice. My expertise in urban housing and land policies is fueled by an unwavering dedication to uplift the most vulnerable in our society.

My Approach:

  • Empowerment through Research: I dive deep into the intricacies of urban environments, harnessing advanced analytical tools like STATA, R, SQL, and Atlas.ti to craft policies that foster equitable living conditions for all.
  • Sustainable and Cost-Effective Living:
    • Energy Transition: Advocating for the integration of renewable energy solutions, such as solar panels and geothermal systems, to reduce energy costs and elevate air quality for residents.
    • Environmental Considerations: Designing with green roofs, rain gardens, and sustainable features to reduce ecological footprints and protect communities from climate extremes.
    • Cost Reduction: Focusing on low-impact materials and efficient construction methods to ensure affordability without compromising quality.
  • Resilient and Inclusive Communities: I push for policies guaranteeing safe, sustainable, and affordable housing. My vision extends to creating vibrant neighborhoods with community centers, green spaces, and local markets.
  • Advocacy and Action: Actively engaging with community groups, NGOs, and policymakers to challenge systemic housing inequalities and develop inclusive urban plans.
  • Innovation in Housing Solutions: Pioneering co-housing and modular constructions for adaptable, cost-effective housing solutions.
  • Educational Outreach: Empowering communities through education to adopt and maintain sustainable practices, ensuring accessibility and practicality.

The Five A's of Housing:

  • Accessible: Ensuring housing is reachable for all, regardless of socio-economic status.
  • Acceptable: Guaranteeing homes meet high standards of livability and dignity.
  • Adaptable: Designing spaces that evolve with changing resident needs.
  • Affordable: Ensuring cost does not prohibit access to decent housing.
  • Available: Increasing housing supply where it's needed most.

Join me in my mission to redefine urban living – where justice, sustainability, and opportunities converge. Let’s transform our cities into symbols of equity, resilience, and environmental stewardship.
