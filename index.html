What I'm Doing

Research and Analysis

Gather and analyze data on demographic, economic, and environmental factors to understand current conditions and future trends in a region.

Land Use Planning

Develop land use plans and zoning regulations to guide the growth and development of communities. This involves determining where residential, commercial, industrial, and recreational areas should be located.

Community Engagement

Engage with community members, stakeholders, and elected officials to gather input and support for planning initiatives. Public meetings, surveys, and workshops are common methods used to involve the community in decision-making processes.

Policy Development

Draft policies and regulations related to land use, housing, transportation, and environmental protection. These policies are designed to achieve long-term community goals and objectives.

Environmental and Sustainability

Assess the environmental impact of development projects and promote sustainable practices such as green building design, renewable energy use, and conservation of natural resources.

Transportation Planning

Work on transportation systems, including roads, public transit, bike lanes, and pedestrian pathways, to improve accessibility and reduce traffic congestion.
